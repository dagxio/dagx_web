Ecosystem

DAGX is a value exchange network based on DAG. DAGX is a new generation of distributed ledger technology (DLT) based on directed acyclic graph. "X" represents "value interconnect and exchange". DAGX employs the latest blockchain technology to empower various industries including insurance, healthcare, games, entertainment and others to complete asset on-chain and value exchange.

DAG-based value exchange network

The DAGX team believes that the next-generation value Internet will be a multi-dimensional and multi-chain network ecosystem, just like the prosperous biological world. Current industry mainstream experts still infer from traditional history. They believe that in the future, DLT ecological development will be similar to operating systems, and only 3-4 mainstream blockchains will continue to develop. The DAGX team has a far more diverse outlook for the future: Blockchain is bringing about revolutionary changes in production relations, realizing the restructuring of value interconnection and circulation exchange systems. Multiple value exchange networks and distributed multi-dimensional logic function chains in the world conduct asset value interconnection and circulation exchange, which can build a new and multidimensional multi-chain new world.

DAGX team creatively proposes DAGX Value Layers. The DAGX Networks is composed of logical functions with different levels of hierarchical functions, and actively promotes the application of multi-dimensional and multi-chain next-generation value Internet.


DAGX Public Chain 1.0 has been developed and made on-line test. It has also been applied to many industries such as healthcare and insurance technology. Relying on the rich medical and health resources of Ch-gemstone Capital and other partners, DAGX focuses on “healthcare and insurance, mutual assistance” industry, and builds the digital asset ecosystem of the health and insurance industry. In addition, it promotes the realization of asset on-chain, value interconnection and exchange among enterprises in various industries.
